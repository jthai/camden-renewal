Local response to coronavirus

Coronavirus has taken hold at a societal level across the UK. More than ever, we need a new approach to addressing these challenges in a way that is truly built to scale, to make change happen at the speed we need it to.

Working collaboratively with partners at a local and national level, we're investing in a new approach. Bringing together a catalyst response team from local public services, community groups and voluntary services for collective action, we're looking at the most urgent challenges, building on previous work and coordinating an agile response to address shared problems during an emergency unlike any other.
